weekly snaps 4

Date 08/Aug/2012 Tags photo

This week’s post has been delayed by some very expected news.

I’ll start off with two variations of a similar photo I published in Weekly snaps #3; the first is a classical black and white, the second a slightly toned black and white.

Then I have three photos which were the first ones shot with the Nikon D7000; they were very poor in terms of exposure accuracy but then I was very surprised at the amount of stuff I could recover in post processing; a truly wonderful sensor inside the D7000.

Finally a snowy landscape which may remind you that “winter is coming”.

Milano, via Forcella, Nikon D7000, 50mm f/1.4.

Abbazia di Chiaravalle, Milano, Nikon D7000, 35mm f/1.8 and Tokina 12-24mm f/4.

Abandoned railway tracks, Nikon D7000, 35mm f/1.8.

Belvedere Langhe, Nikon D7000, 35mm f/1.8.
