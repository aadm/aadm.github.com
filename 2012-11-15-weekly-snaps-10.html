weekly snaps 10

Date 2012-11-15 Tags photo

It just happened that all the past “weekly snaps” have featured photos linked by some sort of thread or connection. But my original idea was to collect random shots as I wrote at the beginning.

So today I was resolved in finding a few shots that I liked, with nothing to link them; I went from folder to folder in my library, and selected these shots.

However, I now realize that even all of these photographs are joined by something; it’s a bit obscure, and unless you are a bit of a naturalist, you might not find it though (especially for that brown rocky patch)1.

Corfu/Kérkyra, Greece. Nikon D70s, 28mm f/2.8.

Somewhere in the Pyrenees, Spain. Nikon D70s, 50mm f/1.8.

Galway, Ireland. Nikon D70s, 50mm f/1.8.

Going to Barcelona. Nikon D70s, 28mm f/2.8.

Lisbona, Portugal. Panasonic GF1, 17mm f/2.8.

  1. It’s water of course. That brown and green photo from Spain above? An ancient river bed, obviously. ↩
