Date 2012-09-22 Tags photo

Random photos this time. Some are very old, and I can’t even recall which lens I used. The last two are reminders that today was Giulia’s birthday (and this year there was nothing really which could compete with that special gift we’re enjoying together since the 4th of August).

Lulworth Cove (UK). Nikon F70, 35mm f2(?), Kodak Tri-X 400.

City Bikers, Millenium Bridge, London. Nikon F70, 50mm f1.8(?), Kodak Tri-X 400.

Milano, after a snowfall. Nikon D70s, 50mm f/1.8.

Beijing. Nikon D70s, Sigma 17-70 f/2.8-4.5 @ 17mm.

Giulia’s birthday. iPhone 4S, Hueless.

The best gift ever. Nikon D7000, 50mm f/1.4.
