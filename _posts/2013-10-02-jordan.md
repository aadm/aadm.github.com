A selection of photographs taken during a recent trip to Jordan in the middle of September 2013.

I tried to capture something of the Dead Sea and then on my last day I went to Petra -- because you can't really go to Jordan and not visit Petra.

I will have to go back someday and bring my family because swimming in the Dead Sea is really an unreal experience.

Petra, well I'm not too sure I enjoyed it but maybe because I don't like dead things and places; I probably enjoyed more the hiking but if I want to hike I can go someplace closer.
